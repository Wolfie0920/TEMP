MISYON ALAGA

EVERYONE MATTERS

No child should suffer from hunger or hidden hunger. Through care, nutrition, and education, we stand to end malnutrition — one child, one community at a time.

Our Vision

We envision a Philippines where every child grows up nourished, healthy, and full of potential - supported by communities that value well-being, dignity, and hope. Our vision is a nation free from malnutrition, built by empowered families, compassionate youth leaders, and resilient communities, one community at a time.

Our Mission

Misyon ALAGA PH is devoted to combating childhood malnutrition through sustainable nutrition programs, health education, and community-based interventions. We seek to empower families and uphold the well-being of Filipino children. With compassion and purpose, we aim to transform lives — one community at a time — while inspiring the youth to take action and pay it forward for generations to come.

Our Goal

Our primary goal is to significantly reduce childhood malnutrition in underserved areas through strategic partnerships, inclusive health and nutrition policies, and localized, sustainable programs. Through this movement, we aim not only to uplift children and families — one community at a time — but also to awaken a generation of young advocates who will continue the mission and extend its impact to future generations.

Core Values

Compassion: We serve with empathy and care, recognizing the daily struggles of families affected by poverty and hunger.

Integrity: We uphold transparency, accountability, and ethical responsibility in all aspects of our work.

Empowerment: We equip individuals and communities — especially the youth — with the tools, knowledge, and confidence to drive their own transformation.

Equity: We believe that every child deserves access to proper nutrition and care, regardless of background or circumstance.

Sustainability: We prioritize long-term impact by building programs that endure and inspire action beyond our direct involvement.

Hope: We cultivate resilience and optimism, affirming every child’s right to dream, grow, and succeed.

Community-Centered Service: We commit to meaningful, localized impact by listening to, working with, and growing alongside the people we serve — one community at a time — while nurturing the next generation of changemakers.
